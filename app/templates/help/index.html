Feature Guide

Learn about all the tools available in Sovereign to support your recovery journey.

Dashboard

Your personal command center. The dashboard provides an overview of your progress including current streaks, recent activity, mood trends, and quick access to all features. It's designed to give you a quick snapshot of how you're doing each day.

Habits

Build positive routines by tracking daily habits. Create habits you want to maintain—exercise, meditation, reading, or any activity that supports your recovery. Mark them complete each day to build consistency and track your streaks over time.

  • Create habits with custom frequencies
  • Track daily completions
  • View streak history

Relapse Tracking

Track relapse events to understand patterns and triggers. Logging relapses helps you identify what leads to them and how to prevent future occurrences. View statistics to see your progress over time and identify improvement areas.

  • Log relapse events with details
  • Track severity levels
  • View statistics and trends

Journal

A personal diary for your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. Writing helps process emotions, reflect on your day, and track your mental state. Review past entries to see how far you've come in your recovery journey.

  • Create dated journal entries
  • Write freely about your experiences
  • Review and edit past entries

Mood Tracker

Track your emotional state daily. Regular mood logging helps identify patterns between your mood and other factors like sleep, activities, or triggers. View trends over time to better understand what affects your wellbeing.

  • Log daily mood with notes
  • View mood trends over time
  • Identify emotional patterns

Triggers

Identify and track things that trigger cravings or urges. By recognizing your triggers, you can prepare strategies to handle them. Track how often you encounter triggers and how many times you've successfully overcome them.

  • Create and categorize triggers
  • Track encounters and victories
  • Build awareness of patterns

Achievements

Gamify your recovery with achievements. Earn badges and milestones for reaching goals like maintaining streaks, logging entries consistently, or hitting recovery milestones. These rewards help motivate you on your journey.

  • Earn badges for accomplishments
  • Track milestone achievements
  • Stay motivated with rewards

Consistency Builder

Focus on building one habit at a time with the consistency builder. Rather than tracking multiple habits, concentrate on a single area of improvement. Track your daily completions and watch your consistency percentage grow.

  • Focus on one habit at a time
  • Track completion percentage
  • Build lasting consistency

Addiction Killer

Access techniques and strategies to combat addiction urges. Create custom "killers"—specific actions or techniques you can use when faced with a craving. Document what works and rate effectiveness to build your personal toolkit.

  • Create personal coping techniques
  • Use techniques when cravings hit
  • Rate and improve strategies

Calendar

Visualize your progress with a monthly calendar view. See at a glance which days you completed your habits. This bird's-eye view helps identify patterns and keeps you motivated to maintain consistency.

  • View habits on a monthly calendar
  • Navigate between months
  • See completion patterns

Export Data

Download your data in CSV format for backup or analysis. Export your habits, journal entries, mood logs, relapse records, or get a complete data dump. Your data belongs to you.

  • Export habits as CSV
  • Download journal entries
  • Get complete data backups
